Product Mission

Use machine learning to recognize food images and estimate weights, and give prediction of its calories. Calorie Predictor doesn't need users to know the weight of foods or enter names of foods, all it needs is a picture.

group

User Stories

<1> I, the college student who try to keep on diet in order to lose weights.
<2> I, the professional bodybuilder, who try to keep very strict diet before my show.
<3> I, the patient, who have health problem needed to avoid eating high calorie foods, need to know the energy of foods.

settings

MVP

1. An user interface that allow users to input foods images.
2. Capability of recognizing foods and measuring weights.
3. Calculate calories based on calorie table
